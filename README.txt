== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is the purpose of this plugin? =

This plugin simplifies the integration and management of different advertisement types on WordPress sites, enhancing content monetization through easy-to-manage ad placements.

Justbaat plugin relies on a third-party service to enhance its functionality. This service is integral to providing the best possible user experience and ensuring the smooth operation of the plugin.

Use of Third-Party Services

Data Transmission
